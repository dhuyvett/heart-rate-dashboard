# Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 15, 2026

Heart Rate Dashboard is designed to keep your data private.

## Summary

- All app data remains on your device.
- Data is stored in an encrypted local database.
- No workout, heart rate, or location data is uploaded to any server.
- No data is sold or shared with third parties.
- No account is required.

## Information We Process

The app may process the following data.  All data processing occurs within the app on your device:

- Heart rate readings from your connected Bluetooth heart rate monitor
- Session details (for example, session name, start/end time, summary stats)
- Optional GPS-based speed, distance, and altitude data (only when enabled by you)
- App settings and preferences

## How We Use Data

Your data is used only to provide core app functionality, including:

- Real-time heart rate display
- Workout/session recording
- Session history and statistics
- Optional speed and distance tracking

## Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database.
- We do not operate cloud storage for your data.
- We do not transmit your personal or health data to external services.

## Data Sharing and Transfers

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not share your data with advertisers, analytics providers, or data brokers.
- We do not transfer your data off-device.

## Permissions

The app may request permissions needed for features you use:

- Bluetooth permissions to connect to heart rate monitors
- Location permissions for optional GPS speed/distance features

Permissions can be managed in your device settings at any time.

## Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted in this file with a new effective date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app publisher through the support contact listed in the app store listing.
